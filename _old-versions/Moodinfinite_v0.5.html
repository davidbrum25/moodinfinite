Moodinfinite Help

Welcome!

Moodinfinite is a digital canvas for your ideas. Add images, text, arrows, and more to create moodboards, brainstorm, or organize your thoughts.

Core Concepts

  • Panning: Middle-click and drag, or hold Space and left-click and drag to move around the canvas.
  • Zooming: Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
  • Selecting: Click on an item to select it. Hold Shift and click to select multiple items. Click and drag on an empty area to create a selection box.
  • Moving: Click and drag a selected item to move it.

Tools (Left Toolbar & Hotkeys)

  • New Board (Shift+N): Clears the canvas.
  • Add Image (I): Opens a file dialog to add images. You can also paste images or drag-and-drop them onto the canvas.
  • Add Text (T): Click and drag to create a text box. Double-click to edit.
  • Add Arrow (A): Click and drag to draw an arrow.
  • Add Box (B): Click and drag to create a colored rectangle.
  • Draw (D): Freehand drawing tool.
  • Eyedropper (E): Pick a color from the canvas to set as your accent color.
  • Auto Align (Shift+A): Neatly arranges two or more selected items into a grid.

Selection Toolbar

When you select an item, a floating toolbar appears with context-specific actions.

  • Text Controls: Change font, alignment, and style (bold/italic) for selected text items.
  • Grouping (Ctrl+G): Combine multiple selected items into a single group.
  • Ungrouping (Ctrl+Shift+G): Break a group back into its individual items.
  • Layering (Page Up/Down): Bring items to the front or send them to the back.
  • Scale (S): Activate the scale handle on the bottom-right of an item. Hold Shift to maintain aspect ratio while scaling.
  • Rotate (R): Activate the rotate handle on the top-right of an item. Hold Shift to snap to 15-degree increments.
  • Pin (P): Lock an item in place to prevent accidental movement or editing.
  • Delete (Del): Remove the selected item(s).

Other Hotkeys

  • Copy: Ctrl+C
  • Cut: Ctrl+X
  • Paste: Ctrl+V
  • Duplicate: Ctrl+D
  • Undo: Ctrl+Z
  • Redo: Ctrl+Shift+Z
  • Select All: Ctrl+A
  • Toggle Grid: G
  • Open Project: Ctrl+O
  • Save Project: Ctrl+S
  • Export as PNG: Shift+S
